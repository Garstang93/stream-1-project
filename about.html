A brief History

Clive Garstang always had a drive for success, starting work at the young age of 16. He always wanted to work for himself, so he learned as much as possible about the building and plastering trade. In 1984 he had gained the skills and knowledge to start up his own company and began working for himself, building up his reputation with his friendly customer service and his attention to quality. Until now he has only worked off recomendations, starting locally and slowly broadining his work all over Essex. His reputation has led to meeting and doing contract work for a large conservatory company based in Braintree, as well as plenty of private work from great recomendations from our customers.

Where We Work

A lot of our work is based in Harlow, but we have also had many customers around Essex in places such as Braintree, Bishop's Stortford, Basildon and Nazeing. We also take on jobs further away depending on our availability, but we hate to let our customers down so we'll always try to fit you in!
